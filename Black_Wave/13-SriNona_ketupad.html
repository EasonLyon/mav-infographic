The Ketupat Challenge: Pain Points & Opportunities

Consumer Insights

  • Time Constraints

    75% of Malaysians find festive cooking, including ketupat preparation, highly time-consuming.

  • Inconsistent Quality

    Many report instant ketupat can be too hard, mushy, or have an undesirable "plasticky" taste/smell from packaging.

  • Desire for Authenticity & Naturalness

    Growing demand for convenient foods that are also authentic, natural, and free from excessive preservatives.
